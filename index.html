Kaper Walls

A large spot with a lot of jumps, great for creating short and longer lines!

  • Location: Kapermolen Bridge
  • Category: Flow (Big Jumps)

DUSART ROOFGAP

A great roofgap to jump or flip!

  • Location: Dusartplein
  • Category: Roofgap

Medical School

The most iconic spot in Hasselt. It has a lot of challenges and is perfect for creating lines!

  • Location: In between Dusartplein and Leopoldplein
  • Category: precision, balance and flow

Cultural Center Hasselt

This spot is great for practising Flips and doing Tricking! It is on of the only spots with good grass in the city.

  • Location: Next to the Virga Jesse Hospital.
  • Category: Tricking, Flips

Cursa Walls

The cursa wall are very simmilar to the Medical School; yet it has it own distinct style. Great for finding creative flows and challenging youre friends.

  • Location: The Cursa Campus
  • Category: Flow, precisions

Medical School Wall

Of to the side of the medical school there is a wall that's perfect for practising climb ups and flipping of of.

  • Location: In between Dusartplein and Leopoldplein.
  • Category: Climb ups
